Mahjong Direction

How to play-

Select tiles in matching sets untill all sets are removed from the board.

Rules-

-Two tiles match if they are the same kind of tile and if their ranks are equal:
For example, a Bamboo 6 tile matches any of the other three Bamboo 6 tiles, but it
does not match a Bamboo 5 nor a Circle 6.

-Any two season tiles match as do any two flower tiles.
